# Social and Collaboration Features

## Before you start
* You must have an active account and be logged into the dashboard.
* You need access to the relevant workspace and project to collaborate with your team.

## Steps

1. **Leave a Comment and Mention Teammates**
   Navigate to the task or project page and click the **Comment** button. Type your message in the text box. To notify a teammate directly, use an `@` mention by typing `@` followed by their username.

2. **Attach Files**
   While writing a comment, click the **Attach File** button. Supported file formats are `.pdf`, `.jpg`, `.png`, `.docx`, and `.csv`. The maximum file size limit is 50MB per attachment.

3. **Manage Notification Preferences**
   To adjust notifications for a specific thread, click **Thread Options** in the top right corner of the comment thread. Select **Mute Notifications** to stop receiving updates for that thread, or select **Follow Thread** to ensure you receive alerts for all new activity.

## Expected result
When you complete these steps, your comments and attachments will be visible in the thread. When someone is mentioned using an `@` mention, they will receive an immediate in-app notification and an email alert containing a direct link to your comment.
