# Configuring Notifications

## Overview
Configure your signal-to-noise ratio by adjusting which events trigger notifications and where they are delivered. By the end of this guide, you will understand the different notification channels and how to manage noisy projects or threads.

::: info
By default, you are automatically subscribed to in-app notifications and email digests for all projects you join. Push notifications are disabled by default.
:::

## Prerequisites
* You must have an active user account and be logged into the platform.

## Steps

1. **Navigate to Notification Settings**
   Go to your user profile menu and select **Settings > Notifications**. This dashboard controls your global preferences.

2. **Understand Notification Channels**
   Review the differences between the available delivery methods:
   * **In-app:** These appear in the notification center within the application. They are enabled by default for all direct mentions and thread replies.
   * **Email:** These are delivered to your registered email address. By default, you receive daily email digests of missed activity.
   * **Push:** These are system-level alerts on your device (desktop or mobile). They are disabled by default and must be explicitly enabled per device.

3. **Mute Specific Projects or Threads**
   To silence noisy contexts without leaving them:
   * Open the specific project or thread.
   * Click the **More Options** menu in the top right.
   * Select **Mute Notifications**. You will no longer receive alerts for this context unless explicitly @mentioned.

4. **Troubleshoot Missing Emails**
   If you are not receiving emails (e.g., 'Not receiving emails'):
   * Check your spam, junk, or promotions folder.
   * Ensure your email address in **Settings > Account** is verified.
   * Verify that you haven't globally disabled email notifications in **Settings > Notifications**.

::: tip
You can configure a custom schedule for email digests to only receive them during business hours.
:::

## Expected Result
Your notification preferences will immediately update. You can verify this by checking the toggle states in **Settings > Notifications**, which should reflect your new choices.
