# Managing Tasks and Issues

Understand how to effectively manage your day-to-day work by creating, organizing, and tracking tasks.

## Before you start

* Ensure you have access to a Project workspace.
* You need appropriate permissions to create and edit tasks within the project.

## Steps

### Creating and Assigning Tasks

Creating detailed tasks helps your team understand what needs to be accomplished and who is responsible.

1. Navigate to the **Tasks** board in your project sidebar.
2. Click the **New Task** button at the top right of the screen.
3. Enter a clear and descriptive title in the **Task Title** field.
4. Add details in the **Description** box to clarify the scope of work.
5. Click the **Assignee** dropdown to assign the task to yourself or a team member.
6. Click the **Due Date** selector to establish a deadline for the work.
7. Click **Create** to save and add the task to your project.

### Updating Task Statuses

Keep your team informed about work progress by moving tasks through different stages.

1. Open your project's **Tasks** board to view all current work items.
2. Locate the task you want to update.
3. Click and hold the task card, then drag it to the appropriate status column, such as **To Do**, **In Progress**, or **Done**.
4. Alternatively, click the task to open its details, and select the new status from the **Status** dropdown menu.

### Using Labels and Filters

Organize large amounts of work and quickly find specific tasks using categorization and filtering tools.

1. Open a task's details by clicking on it.
2. Click the **Add Label** button and select relevant categories (e.g., bug, feature, urgent) to classify the work.
3. To find specific work items, click the **Filter** button at the top of the **Tasks** board.
4. Select the labels, assignees, or statuses you want to view to narrow down the visible tasks.
5. Click **Clear Filters** when you want to return to viewing all tasks.

## Expected result

You should be able to create tasks with clear ownership and deadlines, track their progress through your workflow, and easily organize or locate specific work items using labels and filters.
