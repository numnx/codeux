# Invite Team Members

This guide explains how to invite your team members to the workspace.

## Prerequisites

- You must have **Admin** or **Owner** access to invite new team members.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Members** in your dashboard.
2. Click on the **Invite Member** button.
3. Enter the email address of the team member you wish to invite.
4. Select the appropriate role for the user (see Roles below).
5. Click **Send Invite**.

## Expected Result

Once the invite is sent, the user will appear in your **Members** list with a "Pending" status until they accept the invitation.

## Roles

When inviting a team member, you can assign them one of the following roles:

- **Admin**: Has full access to manage the workspace, including billing and user management.
- **Member**: Can create, edit, and manage projects and resources but cannot access billing or workspace settings.
- **Viewer**: Read-only access. Can view projects and resources but cannot make any changes.

For deeper reading on what each role can do, see the detailed [Roles and Permissions](../reference/roles-and-permissions.md) page.
