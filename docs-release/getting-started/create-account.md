# Create an Account

## Overview
This guide will walk you through the process of registering a new account and verifying your email address.

::: info
You need a valid email address that you have access to in order to complete the verification process.
:::

## Before you start
* A supported web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
* Access to your email inbox

## Steps

1. **Navigate to Sign Up**
   Go to the application homepage and click the **Sign Up** button located in the top right corner of the screen.

2. **Enter Credentials**
   Fill in your email address, a strong password, and any other required details in the form. Click **Submit** or **Register** to proceed.

3. **Verify Email**
   Check your email inbox for a verification link sent from our system. Click the **Verify My Account** button or link in that email to activate your account.

## Expected Result
You should see a confirmation message stating your email has been verified, and you should be able to log in with the credentials you provided.

## Troubleshooting

### No verification email received
If you did not receive a verification email:
* Check your Spam or Junk folder.
* Ensure you entered the correct email address during sign-up.
* Wait a few minutes; sometimes delivery can be delayed.
* Go back to the login page and click **Resend Verification Email** if the option is available.

## Next Steps
Once your account is verified, you can proceed to the [Overview](../index.md) to explore the system.
