# Creating Your First Project

Welcome! This guide will walk you through creating your very first project in the system.

## Prerequisites
- You must be logged into your account.

## Steps to Create a Project

1. Click on the **Create project** button located on your dashboard.
2. Enter a descriptive name for your new project in the name field.
3. Choose the visibility setting (e.g., Public or Private) that best suits your needs.
4. Click the confirm or submit button to finalize creation.

## Expected Result
Upon successful creation, you will be immediately redirected to your new project's dashboard.
