# Configure Workspace Settings

::: warning
These actions require Owner or Admin permissions.
:::

## Overview
This guide explains how to manage general settings for your workspace. By the end of this guide, you will be able to update your workspace logo, set the default timezone and language, and manage email domain restrictions for automatic joining.

## Prerequisites
* Owner or Admin permissions for the workspace.

## Steps

1. **Navigate to Workspace Settings**
   Log into your dashboard, click on **Settings** in the main menu, and select **Workspace Settings**.

2. **Update the Workspace Logo**
   Under the **General** section, find the current logo and click **Update Logo**. Select an image file from your computer and upload it.

3. **Set Timezone and Default Language**
   Scroll to the **Preferences** section. Select the appropriate **Timezone** and **Default Language** from the dropdown menus.

4. **Restrict Email Domains for Automatic Joining**
   In the **Access & Security** section, locate the **Domain Restrictions** field. Enter the specific email domains (e.g., `@example.com`) that are permitted to automatically join the workspace and click **Add Domain**.

5. **Save Changes**
   Click the **Save Changes** button at the bottom of the page to apply all updates.

## Expected Result
Your workspace settings will immediately reflect the new logo, timezone, language preferences, and domain restrictions. You should see a success notification confirming the updates.
