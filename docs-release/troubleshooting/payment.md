# Payment Troubleshooting

## Overview
We're here to help! This guide will assist you in resolving common payment-related issues, such as dealing with declined charges, unlocking a suspended account, or updating your tax information on past invoices. Please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team if you need further assistance.

::: info
All account payment configurations and billing histories are securely processed and managed by our payment provider (Stripe).
:::

## Common Decline Codes

When a payment fails, your bank or Stripe may provide a specific decline code. Here are some of the most common codes and how you can address them:

| Decline Code | Meaning | What to do |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `insufficient_funds` | There are not enough funds in the account to cover the charge. | Please add funds to your account or provide an alternative payment method. |
| `card_declined` | The card has been declined for an unknown reason by the bank. | Contact your bank to authorize the charge, or try a different card. |
| `expired_card` | The card used has passed its expiration date. | Update your payment details with a new, valid card. |
| `incorrect_cvc` | The security code entered does not match the card. | Re-enter your payment information, ensuring the CVC is correct. |
| `processing_error` | An error occurred while processing the card. | Please wait a moment and try the transaction again. |

## Locked Accounts Due to Non-Payment

If an invoice remains unpaid for an extended period, access to your account may be temporarily locked. Don't worry—getting back up and running is straightforward.

1. **Check Your Email:** You will receive a notification with a secure link to view your outstanding balance.
2. **Update Payment Info:** Click the link to update your payment method or retry the current card.
3. **Automatic Unlock:** Once the outstanding balance is successfully paid, your account will unlock automatically within a few minutes.
4. **Need Help?:** If your account remains locked after payment, please reach out to support.

## Updating VAT/Tax IDs on Past Invoices

If you forgot to include your VAT or Tax ID, or if it has changed, you can easily update this on previous invoices.

## Prerequisites
* You must have Owner or Billing Admin permissions for your workspace.
* Have your new VAT/Tax ID ready.

## Steps

1. **Navigate to Billing Settings**
   Log in to your dashboard and go to **Settings** > **Billing**.

2. **Access Invoice History**
   Scroll down to the **Invoice History** section and click on the invoice you need to update.

3. **Edit Invoice Details**
   Click the **Edit** button next to your company address or Tax ID information.

4. **Enter New VAT/Tax ID**
   Update the field with your correct VAT/Tax ID.

5. **Save Changes**
   Click **Save**. The invoice will automatically regenerate with the updated information.

## Expected Result
When you view or download the updated invoice, your new VAT/Tax ID will be clearly visible in the billing details section.
