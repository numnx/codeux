# Troubleshooting Permissions and Access

If you cannot see a project or if certain buttons are disabled, you likely lack the required permissions.

::: info
App support cannot grant you access to internal projects. You must contact your internal team to resolve permission issues.
:::

## Before you start

Understanding how permissions are structured can help you request the correct access from your team:

* **Workspace**: The highest level, acting as the billing and organizational boundary.
* **Project**: The collaboration boundary within a Workspace.
* **Task**: The fundamental units of execution within a Project.

Access is inherited downwards. However, access to a Workspace does not automatically grant access to all its Projects.

## Steps

If you need access to a resource or feature:

1. **Identify your internal administrator**
   Determine who in your organization manages the Workspace. This is typically a manager, team lead, or IT administrator.

2. **Request access directly**
   Contact your internal administrator rather than reaching out to app support.

3. **Specify the required access level**
   Inform your administrator whether you need access to a specific Project, a Task, or the entire Workspace, so they can assign the correct permissions.

4. **Address billing access (Edge Case)**
   If you are an invited guest trying to change billing settings, note that guests are restricted from modifying billing. You must ask a Workspace Admin to make these changes for you.

## Expected result

By following these steps, you will route your access request to the correct internal administrator who can grant you the necessary permissions.
