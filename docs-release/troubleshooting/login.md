# Fix Login Issues

## Before you start
Use this guide to regain access to the app.
Find your issue below for a rapid fix.

## Steps

1. **Find your problem and apply the fix**
   Review the table below to resolve your login issue.

   | Problem | Likely Cause | Fix |
   | --- | --- | --- |
   | Expired magic link | The login link timed out. | Return to the **Login** page and request a new link. Use it within 15 minutes. |
   | Lost 2FA device | You do not have your authenticator app. | Enter a recovery code. Click **Use Recovery Code** on the 2FA verification screen. |
   | SSO misconfiguration | Workspace identity settings are incorrect. | Ask your workspace admin to check SSO settings. |

2. **Contact support**
   If you still cannot log in, contact support.
   Provide the exact required information payload to get help faster.

   Required payload:
   * Your account email address
   * Your workspace URL
   * The exact error message on the screen
   * The date and time of the failed login attempt

## Expected result
You should be logged into the app and see your workspace dashboard.
